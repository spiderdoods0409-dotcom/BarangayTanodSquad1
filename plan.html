Time Management and Planning Techniques

The Pomodoro Technique: Work in focused, 25-minute intervals, followed by a 5-minute break.

Time Blocking: Schedule specific blocks of time in your calendar for certain tasks.

Prioritize Your To-Do List: Rank your tasks by importance and urgency.

Create a Routine: Having a consistent daily routine can train your brain to get to work at certain times.
