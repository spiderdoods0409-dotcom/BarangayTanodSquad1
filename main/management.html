Ways to Finish Tasks

2. Breaking Down Tasks and Setting Goals

The "Swiss Cheese" Method: Break a large, daunting task into small, manageable "holes" that are easy to start and complete. This creates a sense of momentum.

The 2-Minute Rule: If a task takes less than two minutes to complete, do it immediately.

Set SMART Goals: Make your goals Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

"Eat the Frog": Tackle your most difficult or stressful task first thing in the morning.
